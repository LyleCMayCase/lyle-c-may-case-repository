About This Collection

This collection contains publicly available government records, official reports, correspondence, and historical documents relevant to the case of Lyle C. May.

Many of these materials provide historical context regarding the investigation, forensic practices, governmental agencies, and other subjects discussed throughout the Supplemental Motion and Source Verification Archive.

Whenever possible, original government publications and primary source documents are provided to encourage independent review.
