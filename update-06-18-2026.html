State Requests Extension of Time

Recent Developments

Filed • June 18, 2026

Summary

On June 18, 2026, the State of North Carolina filed a Motion for Extension of Time requesting an additional sixty days to comply with the Court's May 11, 2026 Order.

The motion requests additional time to file the State's Answer to the Supplemental Motion for Appropriate Relief and to calendar the matter for hearing, citing the size of the record, the procedural history of the case, and current workload as the basis for the request.


Why This Matters

On May 11, 2026, the Court directed the State to file an Answer to the Supplemental Motion for Appropriate Relief and to calendar the matter for hearing within specified deadlines.

This filing represents the State's request for additional time to comply with those directives. The Court will determine whether the requested extension should be granted.
