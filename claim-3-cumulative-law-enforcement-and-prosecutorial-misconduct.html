Claim III

CUMULATIVE LAW ENFORCEMENT AND PROSECUTORIAL MISCONDUCT

Last Revised • July 2, 2026

Claim III alleges that the cumulative effect of law enforcement and prosecutorial misconduct deprived the Defendant of a fair trial and due process under the United States and North Carolina Constitutions.

Rather than relying upon any single alleged error, this claim argues that the combined constitutional violations, viewed together, resulted in prejudice requiring post-conviction relief. The materials below summarize the principal issues and authorities discussed in Claim III.
