Claim III

CUMULATIVE LAW ENFORCEMENT AND PROSECUTORIAL MISCONDUCT

Last Revised • June 30, 2026

Official Claim Title

CLAIM III. CUMULATIVE LAW ENFORCEMENT AND PROSECUTORIAL MISCONDUCT CAUSED THE DEFENDANT TO BE DENIED A FAIR TRIAL AND DUE PROCESS RIGHTS PROTECTED BY THE FIFTH, SIXTH, AND FOURTEENTH AMENDMENTS TO THE UNITED STATES CONSTITUTION AND ARTICLE I, SECTIONS 19, 23, 24, 27, AND 36 OF THE NORTH CAROLINA CONSTITUTION.

Claim Overview

Claim III alleges that the cumulative effect of law enforcement and prosecutorial misconduct deprived the Defendant of a fair trial and due process under the United States and North Carolina Constitutions. Rather than relying upon any single error, this claim argues that the combined constitutional violations require relief.
