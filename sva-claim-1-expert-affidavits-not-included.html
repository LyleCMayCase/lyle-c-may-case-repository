Cited to Support

May's allegation that expert mental-health professionals concluded the Defendant's age, mental illness, intoxication, and psychological condition at the time of his arrest and interrogation impaired his ability to knowingly, voluntarily, and intelligently waive his constitutional rights or provide a reliable confession. The affidavits are cited in the SMAR as support for the claim that substantial expert evidence exists regarding the Defendant's mental condition and its impact on the voluntariness and reliability of his statements to law enforcement.
