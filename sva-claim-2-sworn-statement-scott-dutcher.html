Sworn Statement of APD Officer Scott R. Dutcher

GOVERNMENT RECORD ENTRY

Last Revised • July 9, 2026

This Source Verification Page documents the sworn statement of Asheville Police Department Officer Scott R. Dutcher, cited in Claim II of the Supplemental Motion. The statement documents Officer Dutcher's observations during the initial response to the reported homicide, his interactions with Darrell Godfrey, and his participation in securing the crime scene.

Cited to Support

May's allegation that APD Officer Scott Dutcher was among the first law enforcement officers to respond to the reported stabbing, interview Darrell Godfrey, investigate the circumstances surrounding the alleged crime, and secure the crime scene at 75 Rock Hill Road. Dutcher's statement is cited to establish Godfrey's initial accusations against the Defendant, Godfrey's uncertainty regarding where the assault occurred, Dutcher's observation of what appeared to be blood inside the residence, and the sequence of events that led to Sgt. Randy Riddle transporting Godfrey to the Blue Ridge Parkway. May relies upon this statement to support his claim that Dutcher possessed material firsthand knowledge concerning Godfrey's initial statements, the condition of the crime scene, and the early stages of the investigation, yet was not called by the State to testify before the jury despite his significant involvement in the case.
