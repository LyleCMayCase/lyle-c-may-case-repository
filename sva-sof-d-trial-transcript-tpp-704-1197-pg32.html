Trial Transcript Entry

Last Revised • July 7, 2026

This Source Verification Page documents portions of the State v. May trial transcript cited in Statement of Facts D of the Supplemental Motion. The cited testimony concerns Detective Judy Romick's preparation of the Defendant's written confession, Detective Forrest Weaver's involvement in the investigation, and testimony regarding the circumstances surrounding the creation of the Defendant's written statement.

Cited to Support

May's allegation that Officer Judy Hensley, a witness who personally observed the Defendant for several hours prior to interrogation, was not called to testify at either the pretrial suppression hearing or trial. The cited transcript passages are relied upon to establish that Detective Judy Romick testified regarding observations attributed to Officer Hensley despite Hensley not being presented as a witness. May relies upon these transcript passages to support his claim that the defense was denied the opportunity to cross-examine Hensley regarding the Defendant's condition, demeanor, and conduct prior to interrogation and that testimony concerning Hensley's observations was instead introduced through Detective Romick.
