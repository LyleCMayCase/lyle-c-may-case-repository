Lyle C. May

BOOKS • ARTICLES • PODCASTS • INTERVIEWS

Last Revised • July 14, 2026

Lyle C. May is a North Carolina author, prison journalist, public speaker, and incarcerated writer currently housed on North Carolina's death row. Throughout his incarceration, he has published books, newsletters, essays, articles, and interviews focusing on education, prison reform, criminal justice, and related issues. His work has appeared in publications, podcasts, and public forums addressing a wide range of topics related to life in prison, higher education, and the criminal legal system.


This collection highlights his publicly available writings, podcasts, interviews, and other documented works. The materials included here are organized for research and reference and are intended to provide additional context regarding his activities and rehabilitation while incarcerated.
