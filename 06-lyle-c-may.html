Lyle C. May

BOOKS • ARTICLES • PODCASTS • INTERVIEWS

Last Revised • July 12, 2026

This collection highlights Lyle C. May's publicly available writings, podcasts, interviews, and other documented works. The materials included here are organized for research and reference and are intended to provide additional context regarding his activities and rehabilitation while incarcerated.


Primary Source

Much of the material contained within this collection originates from Lyle C. May's official website, which remains the primary source for his newsletters, published writing, interviews, and personal updates.


This collection is intended to complement—not replace—the material available through Lyle C. May's official website by providing a research-oriented archive of publicly available publications, interviews, and related materials.


Visitors are encouraged to explore the original website to access additional content, future updates, to join the official mailing list, and for information published directly by Lyle C. May.
