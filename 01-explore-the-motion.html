About This Guide

The Supplemental Motion for Appropriate Relief exceeds 380 pages and contains numerous legal claims, supporting authorities, exhibits, and citations. This guide is intended to help readers navigate the motion by organizing it into its major sections. Each section serves as a starting point for exploring the original filing and the supporting materials available throughout the repository.
