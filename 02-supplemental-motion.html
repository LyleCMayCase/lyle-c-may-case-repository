About This Collection

This collection contains the Supplemental Motion for Appropriate Relief (SMAR) filed on behalf of Lyle C. May, together with any publicly available materials directly associated with that motion.

The motion presents the legal claims, supporting factual allegations, and citations to the authorities relied upon in support of those claims. Readers seeking to review the supporting source material should continue to Repository Collection 02 — Source Verification Archive.

The Supplemental Motion should be read in conjunction with the Source Verification Archive, which organizes and documents the authorities cited throughout the motion.
