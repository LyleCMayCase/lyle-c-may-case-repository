State of North Carolina v. Darrell Elburt Godfrey

COURT RECORD ENTRY

Last Revised • July 9, 2026

This Source Verification Page documents the Buncombe County District Court Register of Actions for State of North Carolina v. Darrell Elburt Godfrey. The court record is cited in Claim II of the Supplemental Motion to document an additional criminal charge involving Darrell Godfrey prior to the July 1997 homicides and to evaluate information bearing upon his credibility as the State's primary witness.

Cited to Support

May's allegation that Darrell Godfrey was charged with Assault on a Female prior to the July 1997 homicides; that the alleged victim was identified as Kelli Buckner; and that evidence of this charge could have been used by the defense in evaluating and challenging Godfrey's credibility had he testified before the jury. May cites this court record to support his claim that the jury was not informed of material information concerning Godfrey's prior criminal history and alleged acts of violence.
