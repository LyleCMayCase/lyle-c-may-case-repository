About This Collection

The Source Verification Archive (SVA) was prepared to accompany the Supplemental Motion for Appropriate Relief. It organizes the publicly available authorities cited throughout the motion into a single, searchable reference collection.

Each entry identifies the cited authority, verifies the source, provides publication information where available, highlights the relevant portions of the original document, and explains why the authority was cited within the Supplemental Motion.

Rather than requiring readers to locate dozens of authorities independently, the archive presents those materials in the same sequence in which they appear throughout the Supplemental Motion, allowing each citation to be reviewed in its original context.
