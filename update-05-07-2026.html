Court Orders State to Respond

Recent Developments

Entered • May 7, 2026 Filed • May 11, 2026

Summary

On May 7, 2026, Judge Marvin P. Pope, Jr. entered an Order directing the State of North Carolina to file an Answer to Lyle C. May's Motion for Appropriate Relief and to calendar the matter for hearing.

The Court found good cause to order a hearing after reviewing the Motion for Appropriate Relief, the Supplement, and the North Carolina Supreme Court's prior appellate decision. The Order directs the State to address specific issues identified by the Court and establishes deadlines for both the State's response and the scheduling of a hearing.


Why This Matters

This Order marks the beginning of the Court's formal consideration of the Supplemental Motion for Appropriate Relief. In addition to directing the State to respond, the Court identified three areas requiring particular attention and established deadlines for the State's Answer and for scheduling a hearing.

The filings and Orders that follow in this collection document the proceedings that occurred after this Order was entered.
