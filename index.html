The Lyle C. May Case Repository is an independently maintained research archive documenting the court records, legal authorities, source materials, and supporting documentation relating to the Supplemental Motion for Appropriate Relief filed pro se by Lyle C. May in January 2026.


Lyle C. May is currently incarcerated on North Carolina's death row and is seeking post-conviction relief through the North Carolina courts.


The repository is organized to provide researchers, journalists, attorneys, students, and members of the public with direct access to the court filings, trial transcripts, legal authorities, and supporting source materials referenced throughout the motion. Wherever possible, materials are presented with citations to their original sources so readers may examine the record for themselves.


New to the repository? Begin with the Start Here section to learn about the repository, how it is organized, the editorial standards used to compile it, and the best way to navigate its collections.
