About This Repository

This repository provides organized access to publicly available court filings, legal authorities, research materials, and supporting documentation relating to the case of Lyle C. May.

Its purpose is to assist attorneys, journalists, legal researchers, educators, students, and members of the public in locating and reviewing publicly available materials associated with the case.

Readers are encouraged to examine the source materials and reach their own conclusions.
