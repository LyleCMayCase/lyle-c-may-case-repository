Statement of Facts: A

STATE BUREAU OF INVESTIGATION

Last Revised • July 2, 2026

This section examines publicly available information relating to the North Carolina State Bureau of Investigation, including independent reviews of SBI laboratory practices, reported forensic irregularities, and allegations concerning the handling and presentation of forensic evidence in the Defendant's case. It also discusses the role of SBI analysts and the significance of forensic evidence to the claims presented in the Supplemental Motion.
