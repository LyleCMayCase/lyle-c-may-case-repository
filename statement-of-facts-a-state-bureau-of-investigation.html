Statement of Facts: A

STATE BUREAU OF INVESTIGATION

Last Revised • July 2, 2026

This section examines publicly available information relating to the North Carolina State Bureau of Investigation, including independent reviews of SBI laboratory practices, reported forensic irregularities, and allegations concerning the handling and presentation of forensic evidence in the Defendant's case.

The materials included here provide context for the forensic issues discussed in the Supplemental Motion and the role of SBI evidence in the claims presented throughout the repository.
