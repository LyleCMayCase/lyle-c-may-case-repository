Repository Overview

Welcome to The Lyle C. May Case Repository, an independently organized research archive documenting the publicly available materials associated with the Supplemental Motion for Appropriate Relief filed on behalf of Lyle C. May.

Its purpose is to organize court records, legal authorities, trial transcripts, research materials, and supporting documentation into a single searchable collection, allowing readers to move easily between the legal arguments presented in the Supplemental Motion and the publicly available sources upon which those arguments rely.

Why This Repository Exists

The legal claims presented in the Supplemental Motion rely upon numerous publicly available court records, published opinions, trial transcripts, statutes, research reports, and other supporting materials. These sources are often scattered across multiple locations and can be difficult to locate, review, and understand together.

This repository was created to organize those materials into one place, making it easier for readers to follow the legal arguments presented in the Supplemental Motion and examine the original authorities supporting those claims.

What You'll Find Here

Within this repository you will find the complete Supplemental Motion for Appropriate Relief, a section-by-section guide explaining each legal claim, the Source Verification Archive containing the authorities cited throughout the motion, downloadable repository documents, and updates documenting significant additions and developments related to the case.

Using the Repository

The repository has been organized so that visitors may explore it in whatever order best suits their interests.

Some readers may wish to begin by reading the Supplemental Motion from beginning to end. Others may prefer to review a specific claim, examine the supporting authorities within the Source Verification Archive, or download the complete repository documents for offline review.

Each collection has been designed to work independently while remaining connected to the broader repository.

Welcome
