Repository Overview

Welcome to The Lyle C. May Case Repository, an independently organized research archive documenting the publicly available materials associated with the Supplemental Motion for Appropriate Relief filed on behalf of Lyle C. May.

Its purpose is to organize court records, legal authorities, trial transcripts, research materials, and supporting documentation into a single searchable collection that connects the Supplemental Motion with the publicly available sources upon which it relies.

Why This Repository Exists

The legal claims presented in the Supplemental Motion rely upon numerous publicly available court records, published opinions, trial transcripts, statutes, research reports, and other supporting materials. This repository brings those materials together into one organized location, making it easier to follow the motion and examine the authorities supporting its claims.

What You'll Find Here

Within this repository you will find the complete Supplemental Motion for Appropriate Relief, a section-by-section guide explaining each legal claim, the Source Verification Archive containing the authorities cited throughout the motion, downloadable repository documents, and updates documenting significant additions and developments related to the case.

Using the Repository

There is no single "correct" place to begin. You may read the Supplemental Motion from beginning to end, explore individual claims, examine the Source Verification Archive, or download the repository documents for offline review.
Each collection has been designed to stand on its own while remaining connected to the broader repository.

Welcome

Whether you are visiting for legal research, education, journalism, or personal interest, we hope this repository makes these publicly available materials easier to locate and examine.
