Media

A Section-by-Section Guide to the Supplemental Motion

Established • 2026

The Media collection documents publicly available news coverage, interviews, podcasts, and other media relating to the case of Lyle C. May.

As additional coverage becomes available, it will be added to this collection and organized for public reference.

Unlike a traditional news article, this repository provides direct access to the publicly available court records, legal authorities, trial transcripts, and supporting documentation cited throughout the Supplemental Motion, allowing reporters and researchers to review the underlying source materials for themselves.

Current Status

At this time, this collection contains only a limited number of publicly available media resources.

The repository will continue to expand as new reporting, interviews, and public discussions become available.

For Journalists & Researchers

The Lyle C. May Case Repository was created to make publicly available court records, legal authorities, trial transcripts, and supporting materials easier to locate and review.

Journalists, documentary producers, podcasters, legal commentators, and researchers are welcome to use this repository as a starting point for their own independent review of the publicly available record.

Every significant case begins with someone willing to examine the record.

Interested in Covering This Case?

Members of the media seeking additional information or wishing to discuss publicly available repository materials are invited to contact the repository administrator at lcmcr.admin@gmail.com
