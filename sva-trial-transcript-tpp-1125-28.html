This Source Verification Page documents a citation to the official trial transcript referenced throughout the Supplemental Motion. Although the cited transcript pages have not yet been obtained for inclusion within the Source Verification Archive, the citations have been preserved to document where testimony appears in the record and how it supports the allegations presented in the Supplemental Motion.

State v. May Trial Transcript

Page 14, Paragraph: 18

Transcript Pages

1125–1128

Source Location

Buncombe County Superior Court Trial Record

Source Status

Trial transcript pages cited throughout the Supplemental Motion but not obtained for inclusion. Transcripts are likely available through counsel, court records, appellate records, or the case file.

Cited to Support

May's allegation that SBI analyst Brenda Bissette provided false or misleading testimony regarding the location of blood evidence recovered during the investigation and that the prosecution failed to correct or clarify that testimony. May cites these portions of the trial transcript to support his claim that testimony concerning the mixture of the Defendant's blood with that of the victims was presented in a manner that obscured the fact that the blood was recovered from clothing worn by Darrell Godfrey on the night of the murders. May further relies upon this testimony to support his allegation that the prosecution facilitated or failed to correct misleading testimony concerning critical forensic evidence.
