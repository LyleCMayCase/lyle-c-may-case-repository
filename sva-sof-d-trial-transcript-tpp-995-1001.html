This Source Verification Page documents portions of the State v. May trial transcript cited in Statement of Facts D of the Supplemental Motion. The cited testimony concerns Detective Judy Romick's preparation of the Defendant's written confession, Detective Forrest Weaver's involvement in the investigation, and testimony regarding the circumstances surrounding the creation of the Defendant's written statement.

Cited to Support

May's allegation that APD Detective Forrest Weaver possessed information relevant to the circumstances surrounding the Defendant's written confession but was not made available for cross-examination during the pretrial suppression hearing. The cited transcript passages are relied upon to establish that Detective Romick testified Weaver witnessed the Defendant writing his statement while she was absent from the room and that Weaver later testified during the Defendant's trial. May relies upon these transcript passages to support his claim that Weaver was a material witness concerning the circumstances under which the written confession was prepared and that his absence from the suppression hearing prevented defense counsel from examining a witness who may have observed the Defendant's condition, level of intoxication, ability to write, and overall capacity during the creation of the statement.
