Welcome

The Lyle C. May Case Repository is an independently organized research archive containing court records, legal filings, source materials, and supporting documentation relating to the Supplemental Motion for Appropriate Relief filed by Lyle C. May.


This collection is intended to help first-time visitors understand how the repository is organized, how documents were sourced, and where to begin their research.


New visitors should start with the materials below before exploring the motion, supporting documentation, and source verification archive.
