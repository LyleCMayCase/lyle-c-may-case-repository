About This Collection

This collection contains publicly available media coverage relating to the case of Lyle C. May, including newspaper articles, magazine features, interviews, podcasts, and other published reporting.

The purpose of this collection is to provide a centralized location for media coverage that may assist readers in understanding the public history of the case and its surrounding issues.

Inclusion within this collection does not imply endorsement of any particular publication or viewpoint. Materials are included to document the publicly available historical record.
