State v. May Trial Transcript

TRIAL TRANSCRIPT ENTRY

Last Revised • July 9, 2026

This Source Verification Page documents the portion of the State v. May trial transcript cited in Claim I of the Supplemental Motion. The cited testimony concerns SBI Agent Brenda Bissette's identification and description of forensic samples collected from Darrell Godfrey's clothing, including evidence introduced as State's Exhibit 134. The testimony is cited to document the source of the biological evidence analyzed by Bissette and its significance to the State's forensic presentation at trial.

Cited to Support

May's allegation that SBI Agent Brenda Bissette identified and described forensic samples collected from Darrell Godfrey's clothing, including cuttings taken from his pants and shirt that were introduced as State's Exhibit 134, and testified that she analyzed all of those samples. The cited testimony is relied upon to establish that Bissette specifically identified the evidence as originating from Darrell Godfrey's clothing before discussing the DNA results associated with those samples. May relies upon this testimony to support his claim that later portions of Bissette's testimony created a misleading impression concerning the source and significance of biological evidence recovered from Godfrey's clothing.
