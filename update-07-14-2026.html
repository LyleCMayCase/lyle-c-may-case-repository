Court Grants State's Motion for Extension of Time

Recent Developments

Entered • July 13, 2026
Filed • July 14, 2026

Summary

On July 13, 2026, Judge Marvin Pope entered an order granting the State's Motion for Extension of Time in response to Lyle C. May's Supplemental Motion for Appropriate Relief.

The order extends the State's deadline to file its Answer until August 24, 2026. The deadline for calendaring the matter for hearing was also extended until October 8, 2026.

The extension was requested by the State to allow additional time to review the Supplemental Motion and prepare its response.


Why This Matters

The order does not address the merits of the Supplemental Motion for Appropriate Relief. Instead, it establishes a revised schedule for the State's response and for future proceedings in the case.

The extension affects the timing of the litigation but does not alter any claims raised in the Supplemental Motion.
