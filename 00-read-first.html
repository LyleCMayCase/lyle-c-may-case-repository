About This Collection

This collection contains the introductory materials for The Lyle C. May Case Repository. Its purpose is to help first-time visitors understand the organization of the repository and how the materials relate to one another before exploring the remaining collections.

The documents included here explain the purpose of the repository, its organizational structure, and the relationship between the Supplemental Motion, the Source Verification Archive (SVA), court filings, public records, and other supporting materials.

Readers who are new to the repository are encouraged to begin here before exploring the remaining collections.
