Sworn Statement of APD Detective Judy Romick

GOVERNMENT RECORD ENTRY

Last Revised • July 7, 2026

This Source Verification Page documents the sworn statement of Asheville Police Department Detective Judy Romick prepared during the investigation of the murders for which Lyle May was convicted. The statement records Detective Romick's involvement in the investigation and the preparation of the Defendant's written confession. Statement of Facts D relies upon this document to identify investigative personnel involved in the Defendant's interrogation and to document Detective Forrest Weaver's participation in the interrogation process.

Cited to Support

May's allegation that APD Detective Forrest Weaver participated in the Defendant's investigation and was present during the preparation of the Defendant's written confession. Detective Judy Romick's statement is cited to establish Detective Weaver's presence during the interrogation process and his role as a witness to the Defendant's written confession. May relies upon this source to document Weaver's direct involvement in the investigation and interrogation of the Defendant and to connect personnel involved in the Walter Bowman investigation with personnel who participated in the Defendant's case.
